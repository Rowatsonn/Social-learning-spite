Thank you for taking part in this study. Before continuing, please read the following information carefully. During the study, you were misled about the nature of the experiment. Now that your participation is completed, these deceptions may now be explained to you. Once you have read this information, if you are happy for your data to be used, you may simply click continue below and proceed to submit the HIT. If you decide you would now like to withdraw from the study, please follow the above steps to submit the HIT but then contact the lead researcher (robin.o.watson@durham.ac.uk) who will arrange for your data to be destroyed.

Throughout the experiment, you were led to believe that you were playing with other real participants. This was not the case. At every stage you were playing with alone with a bot. Therefore, at no point in this experiment have your decisions or behaviour impacted any other participant. The experiment also reported to have randomly allocated you to be the decider in part two. This, again, was untrue, as every real participant who takes part in this study will be assigned this role. This deception was necessary to ensure the integrity of the experimental design and behavioural measures.
