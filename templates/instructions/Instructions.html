Instructions for part 1

During part 1, you will be playing a game with a bot. In this game your task is to earn as many points as you can across 5 rounds. At the start of each round, you will be given 10 points and then must decide how much of this to send to the bot (you will get to keep half of what you don't send).

The bot will then send bonus points back to you, which vary depending on your choice.

When you are ready proceed, click "Begin" below.
